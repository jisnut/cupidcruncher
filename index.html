The "No" Workshop


Thank you for participating in the "No" Workshop!


Before we begin, let's go over some basic points to reassure you that the SEX+STL organization deeply respects your privacy and wishes to ensure this experience feels safe for all participants involved...


  • Your information (name and email address) will NOT be retained for any purposes by the organization after this event. It is only used in the context of this workshop.
  • Your affirmative responses to questions may be retained for statistical curiosities (primarily to improve future iterations of the workshop), but will NOT bare any reference to your personally identifying information (see above).
  • You may elect NOT to have your name OR email address shared with potential matches prior to beginning the workshop.
  • You may absolutlely participate in the "No" workshop without sharing any personal information by NOT answering "yes" to any of the questions.
